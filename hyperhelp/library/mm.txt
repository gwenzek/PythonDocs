%hyperhelp title="Multimedia Services" date="2021-07-11"
*|multimedia-services:⚓|*

The modules described in this chapter implement various algorithms or interfaces
that are mainly useful for multimedia applications.  They are available at the
discretion of the installation.  Here’s an overview:

* |:library/audioop.txt:audioop — Manipulate raw audio data|

* |:library/aifc.txt:aifc — Read and write AIFF and AIFC files|

* |:library/sunau.txt:sunau — Read and write Sun AU files|

  * |:library/sunau.txt/au-read-objects:AU_read Objects|

  * |:library/sunau.txt/au-write-objects:AU_write Objects|

* |:library/wave.txt:wave — Read and write WAV files|

  * |:library/wave.txt/wave-read-objects:Wave_read Objects|

  * |:library/wave.txt/wave-write-objects:Wave_write Objects|

* |:library/chunk.txt:chunk — Read IFF chunked data|

* |:library/colorsys.txt:colorsys — Conversions between color systems|

* |:library/imghdr.txt:imghdr — Determine the type of an image|

* |:library/sndhdr.txt:sndhdr — Determine type of sound file|

* |:library/ossaudiodev.txt:ossaudiodev — Access to OSS-compatible audio devices|

  * |:library/ossaudiodev.txt/audio-device-objects:Audio Device Objects|

  * |:library/ossaudiodev.txt/mixer-device-objects:Mixer Device Objects|
