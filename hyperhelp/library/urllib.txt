"urllib" is a package that collects several modules for working with URLs:

* |:library/urllib.request.txt/module-urllib.request:urllib.request| for opening and reading URLs

* |:library/urllib.error.txt/module-urllib.error:urllib.error| containing the exceptions raised by |:library/urllib.request.txt/module-urllib.request:urllib.request|

* |:library/urllib.parse.txt/module-urllib.parse:urllib.parse| for parsing URLs

* |:library/urllib.robotparser.txt/module-urllib.robotparser:urllib.robotparser| for parsing "robots.txt" files
